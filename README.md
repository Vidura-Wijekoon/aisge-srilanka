# AI Safety Sri Lanka - Resources

Central hub for working-group outputs, event materials, curated reading, and Sri Lanka-specific AI safety, governance, and ethics resources, maintained by the **AI Safety, Governance & Ethics (SGE) Chapter - Sri Lanka**.

> 🇱🇰 *Built to connect Sri Lankan technologists, researchers, civil society, and policymakers with the global AI safety community - and to surface the work already happening in our universities, regulators, and newsrooms.*

**Website:** <https://www.aisgechaptersrilanka.org/>

## 🇱🇰 Local Languages

Sri Lanka-specific. AI safety in Sinhala (සිංහල) and Tamil (தமிழ்) is a frontier problem: most safety tooling, red-teaming corpora, and content moderation classifiers are built for English. We catalogue what exists, what's missing, and where to contribute.

| Resource | Language | Maintainer |
|----------|----------|------------|
| [SinLlama announcement](https://www.newswire.lk/2025/08/27/sinllama-sri-lanka-launches-largest-sinhala-llm-with-10-million-sentences/) | Sinhala | NLP Centre, University of Moratuwa |
| [LK NLP - tools & datasets index](https://lknlp.github.io/) | Sinhala / Tamil | LK NLP community |
| [NLP Centre - Publications](https://www.uom.lk/nlp/publications) | Sinhala / Tamil | University of Moratuwa |
| [Survey on Sinhala NLP tools (arXiv)](https://arxiv.org/html/1906.02358v22) | Sinhala | de Silva et al. |

See [`Local.Languages/`](./Local.Languages) for our running glossary of AI-safety terms in Sinhala and Tamil, plus dataset wishlist.

## 📰 Civil Society

The misinformation/deepfake threat surface is acute in Sri Lanka - gendered deepfakes, election manipulation, and racist AI-generated slop targeting minorities are all documented. This section maps who is fighting back.

| Organization | Focus | Link |
|--------------|-------|------|
| LIRNEasia | Digital regulation, election misinformation | [lirneasia.net](https://lirneasia.net/) |
| Hashtag Generation | Fact-checking, gender & online safety | [hashtaggeneration.org](https://www.hashtaggeneration.org/) |
| FactSeeker (Sri Lanka Press Institute) | Deepfake & image-fabrication debunking | [SLPI](https://slpi.lk/) |
| Watchdog | OSINT, civic tech | [watchdog.team](https://www.watchdog.team/) |
| Right to Life Human Rights Center | Human-rights impact of deepfakes | [right2lifelanka.org](https://www.right2lifelanka.org/) |

See [`Civil.Society/`](./Civil.Society) for incident logs, election-integrity briefs, and digital-rights tracker.
