## Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA)

Sri Lanka's [Personal Data Protection Act No. 9 of 2022](https://www.dataprotection.gov.lk/) is the foundational privacy law that interacts with any AI deployment processing personal data. The Data Protection Authority is the regulator.

## Online Safety Act

The [Online Safety Act No. 9 of 2024](https://www.parliament.lk/) creates an Online Safety Commission with broad powers. Civil-society analysis lives in [`../Civil.Society/Digital.Rights/`](../Civil.Society/Digital.Rights).
