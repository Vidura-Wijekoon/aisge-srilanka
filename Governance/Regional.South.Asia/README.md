## Why this matters for Sri Lanka

- **Cross-border models:** Most LLMs deployed in SL are trained outside; regional alignment reduces compliance friction.
- **Talent flows:** AI workforce mobility (especially India ↔ SL) makes regulatory divergence costly.
- **Regional risk surface:** Election misinformation networks span SL/India/diaspora; harmonised takedown norms help.
