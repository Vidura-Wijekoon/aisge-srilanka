# Digital Rights

Where AI policy meets the rights to privacy, expression, association, and non-discrimination in Sri Lanka.

## Live legal instruments

| Instrument | Year | Status | Notes |
|------------|------|--------|-------|
| Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) No. 9 of 2022 | 2022 | in force | Data Protection Authority operational |
| Online Safety Act No. 9 of 2024 | 2024 | in force, contested | Online Safety Commission with broad powers |
| Computer Crimes Act No. 24 of 2007 | 2007 | in force | predates modern AI |
| ICCPR Act No. 56 of 2007 | 2007 | in force | hate-speech provisions |

## AI-specific rights concerns

1. **Algorithmic decision-making in the public sector** - welfare, taxation, immigration.
2. **Biometric / facial recognition** in policing and infrastructure.
3. **Content moderation** at scale in Sinhala/Tamil - false positives & political pressure.
4. **Predictive policing** as a future risk under digital-economy modernisation.
5. **Worker surveillance** in BPO, garment, and gig sectors.

## How to contribute

Open an issue with a documented rights concern; PR an analysis. We aim to maintain a one-page brief for each major instrument.
