# 📰 Civil Society

The misinformation and deepfake threat surface in Sri Lanka is acute. AI-generated political manipulation, gendered deepfakes targeting women, and racist anti-minority slop have all been documented during 2024-2026. This section maps the actors, the threats, and the policy responses.

## Sub-sections
