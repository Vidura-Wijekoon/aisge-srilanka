# University of Moratuwa

**Department / faculty:** Department of Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) and the cross-university National Languages Processing Centre (NLPC).
**Website:** [cse.mrt.ac.lk](https://cse.mrt.ac.lk/) · [uom.lk/nlp](https://uom.lk/nlp)
**GitHub:** [@nlpcuom](https://github.com/nlpcuom)

## Active research areas

- Sinhala and Tamil natural language processing
- Machine translation (Si-Ta / SiTa v2)
- Large language models for low-resource languages (SinLlama)
- Computational linguistics for Indic scripts

## Notable projects

| Project | Brief |
|---------|-------|
| **SinLlama** | First large-scale Sinhala LLM. Continually pre-trained Llama-3-8B on ~10M Sinhala sentences. Largest Sinhala LLM to date. [Announcement](https://cse.mrt.ac.lk/news/SinLlama) · [paper](https://arxiv.org/abs/2508.09115). |
| **Si-Ta** | Sinhala-Tamil translation system used in production by some SL government organisations. |
| **SiTa v2** | Adds Sinhala ↔ English direction. |
| **Sinhala/Tamil NLP toolkit** | Tokenisers, stemmers, POS taggers - see LKNLP and the NLP Centre publications page. |
