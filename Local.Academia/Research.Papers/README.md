# Research Papers

Curated bibliography of Sri Lanka-authored or Sri Lanka-focused work on AI safety, governance, ethics, NLP, and digital rights.

## Format

We maintain a flat `bibliography.bib` file plus one-paragraph annotations in `annotated.md`. Sort by year, descending.

## Featured

- **de Silva, N. (2019).** *Survey on Publicly Available Sinhala Natural Language Processing Tools and Research.* [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/html/1906.02358v22)
- **LIRNEasia (2022).** *Towards a Realistic AI Policy for Sri Lanka.* [PDF](https://lirneasia.net/wp-content/uploads/2022/01/LIRNEasia-Towards-a-Realistic-AI-Policy-for-Sri-Lanka.pdf)
- **Ministry of Technology (2024).** *Artificial Intelligence in Sri Lanka - White Paper.* [PDF](https://mot.gov.lk/assets/files/AI%20White%20Paper%20March%202024-c09aa49f7990358ad1442103b804511d.pdf)
- **Ministry of Digital Economy (2025).** *AI Sri Lanka 2028 - National Strategy.* [Overview (Digital Watch)](https://dig.watch/resource/sri-lankas-national-strategy-on-ai)
- **LIRNEasia (2024).** *Election Misinformation in Sri Lanka.* [Summary](https://lirneasia.net/2024/11/election-misinformation-in-sri-lanka-report-summary/)
- **Wijeratne, Y. (Watchdog) - selected reports.** Available via [watchdog.team](https://www.watchdog.team/).
- **INSS (2024).** *Sri Lanka in the Age of AI: National Security amid new threats and opportunities.* [INSS](https://www.inss.lk/index.php?id=651)

## How to add a paper

PR with a row in `bibliography.bib` and a 2-4 sentence annotation in `annotated.md`. Indicate whether the paper is open-access.
