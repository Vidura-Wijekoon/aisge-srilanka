# Student Projects

A showcase of Sri Lankan undergraduate and postgraduate projects on AI safety, fairness, NLP, governance, or digital-rights topics. Especially welcome: final-year projects, dissertations, hackathon prototypes.

## Why we collect these

- Surfaces local talent for hiring, mentorship, and research collaboration.
- Helps future students pick non-redundant problems.
- Documents what's already been tried in the SL context.

## Submission template

Create `<year>-<institution-slug>-<project-slug>.md`:

```markdown
**Year:**
**Institution / programme:**
**Authors:**

```
