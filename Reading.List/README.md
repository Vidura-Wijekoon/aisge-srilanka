# 📚 Reading List

A curated, layered reading list. Skim the **Foundational** track first to build vocabulary; then jump to whichever specialist track matches your interest.

## Foundational (start here)

- **Russell, S. (2019).** *Human Compatible.* Penguin.
- **Christian, B. (2020).** *The Alignment Problem.* W.W. Norton.
- **Amodei, D. et al. (2016).** *[Concrete Problems in AI Safety.](https://arxiv.org/abs/1606.06565)* arXiv.
- **Hendrycks, D. (2023).** *[Introduction to AI Safety, Ethics, and Society.](https://www.aisafetybook.com/)* (free online textbook)
- **UNESCO (2021).** *[Recommendation on the Ethics of AI.](https://www.unesco.org/en/artificial-intelligence/recommendation-ethics)*

## Sri Lanka context (essential)

- **Ministry of Digital Economy (2025).** *[AI Sri Lanka 2028 - National Strategy (overview).](https://dig.watch/resource/sri-lankas-national-strategy-on-ai)*
- **Ministry of Technology (2024).** *[AI in Sri Lanka White Paper.](https://mot.gov.lk/assets/files/AI%20White%20Paper%20March%202024-c09aa49f7990358ad1442103b804511d.pdf)*
- **LIRNEasia (2022).** *[Towards a Realistic AI Policy for Sri Lanka.](https://lirneasia.net/wp-content/uploads/2022/01/LIRNEasia-Towards-a-Realistic-AI-Policy-for-Sri-Lanka.pdf)*
- **LIRNEasia (2024).** *[Election Misinformation in Sri Lanka.](https://lirneasia.net/2024/11/election-misinformation-in-sri-lanka-report-summary/)*
- **INSS (2024).** *[Sri Lanka in the Age of AI: National Security.](https://www.inss.lk/index.php?id=651)*

## Governance & policy

- **OECD (2019/2024).** [AI Principles.](https://oecd.ai/en/ai-principles)
- **NIST (2023).** [AI Risk Management Framework 1.0.](https://www.nist.gov/itl/ai-risk-management-framework)
- **EU (2024).** [AI Act.](https://artificialintelligenceact.eu/)
- **UN High-Level Advisory Body on AI (2024).** [Governing AI for Humanity.](https://www.un.org/ai-advisory-body)

## Technical safety

- **Hendrycks, D., Mazeika, M., & Woodside, T. (2023).** *[An Overview of Catastrophic AI Risks.](https://arxiv.org/abs/2306.12001)*
- **Anthropic (2023+).** *[Core Views on AI Safety.](https://www.anthropic.com/news/core-views-on-ai-safety)*
- **OpenAI / DeepMind / Anthropic - model cards & system cards.** Read the actual safety sections.

## Sinhala/Tamil NLP

- **de Silva, N. (2019).** *[Survey on Sinhala NLP.](https://arxiv.org/html/1906.02358v22)*
- **University of Moratuwa NLP Centre.** [Publications page.](https://www.uom.lk/nlp/publications)

## Misinformation, deepfakes, civic tech

- **Wardle, C. & Derakhshan, H. (2017).** *[Information Disorder.](https://rm.coe.int/information-disorder-toward-an-interdisciplinary-framework-for-researc/168076277c)* Council of Europe.
- **First Draft / Bellingcat handbooks.** Practical OSINT.
